Pasta Di Mama

  • Pasta with bacon

  • Pasta Di Mama wholegrain

Ingredients

  • 250 g / 0.5 lb spiral pasta
  • 500 g / 1 lb good Italian sausages
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes / red pepper flakes
  • 800 g / 28 oz crushed tomato
  • Salt and pepper

Preparations

  1. Boil a large pot of water with 2 tsp salt, add pasta.
  2. Use a sharp knife to cut sausages into chunks about 1.5cm/3/5". It will get a bit smeary and the chunks will be weird shapes - don't worry, it self fixes while cooking!
  3. Add baby spinach (if using), pasta, and about 1/2 cup pasta cooking water. Toss quickly, no longer than 1 minute (otherwise the pasta may start to break).
