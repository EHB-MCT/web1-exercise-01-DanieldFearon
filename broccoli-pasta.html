Broccoli Pasta

  • Pasta with bacon
  • Broccoli Pasta
  • Pasta Di Mama wholegrain

Ingredients

  • 350g / 12 oz dried short pasta
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp mixed dried herbs

Preparations

  1. Chop broccoli into small florets.
  2. Place Sauce ingredients in a jar with lid.
  3. Stir vigorously, adding more pasta water if required. Add more salt and pepper if required. Serve immediately, garnished with parmesan.
